What is it?

  Spark Academy camps are all about promoting and spreading awareness, knowledge, and interest in STEAM (Science Technology Engineering Art and Math). Our camps are run by experienced students
from Beaverton Public Schools' FIRST Robotics teams, the Westview Wildcats and School of Science and Technology's Flying Hedgehogs.
   We offer a broad variety of camps, everything from electrical engineering to Computer Aided Design (CAD) to 3D printing. All proceeds
will go towards supporting Beaverton robotics education programs. And, considering Beaverton Public Schools is a 501(c)3 non-profit charity, part of the tuition charged may be eligible for a tax deduction. Please discuss this with your tax preparer or accountant.
   Classes will be held at TVFR Station 67 or TVFR Station 65. All
camps will be a week long with AM camps running from 9 AM to 12 PM
and PM camps will be from 1 PM to 4 PM.
