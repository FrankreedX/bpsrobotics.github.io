2019 Season

The Challenge - DESTINATION: DEEP SPACE Presented by The Boeing Company

In the 2019 game Destination: Deep Space, alliances of 3 compete to use hatch panels (a circular piece of aluminum) to "repair" a cargo ship and 2 rockets, then deliver cargo to those rockets and cargo ship. Teams earn points at the end of the game for each object delivered. Teams also earn point by climbing on a Habitat platform at the end of the game.
