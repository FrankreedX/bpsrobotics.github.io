Bunnybots 2018 | Pack 'Em Up

The Game

Bunnybots is an offseason competition for FIRST Robotics Competition teams hosted by Team 1540, The Flaming Chickens. Bunnybots 2018, Box ‘Em Up, is played on standard carpeted field with two alliances. On each half of the field are 15 plastic crates from FIRST’s 2018 game “Power Up.” There are also 120 red racket balls and 120 blue racket balls placed by teams in the middle third of the field. The goal is to “claim” as many crates as possible by having the obvious majority of the balls in the crates be of your alliance’s color.
